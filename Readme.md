## Business Objective
Analyze regional and customer segment performance to identify high-profit areas and growth opportunities.

## Key Questions
- Which region contributes the highest profit?
- Which segment performs strongest?
- Are there monthly order trends?
- Does higher sales always lead to higher profit?

## Key Findings
- Central region contributes the highest overall profit.
- Corporate segment drives strong performance across regions.
- Order quantity remains relatively stable over time.
- Higher sales do not always result in proportionally higher profit.
- South region may require strategic improvement.

- Tableau
